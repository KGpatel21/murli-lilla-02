Foundation in formation · Est. 2026

Business gives us the means. Community gives us the reason.

The MurliLeela Foundation is being established to carry the group's social commitment — structured, accountable initiatives in education, health, and livelihood for the communities we work alongside.

Areas of Commitment

Three causes, chosen to last.

Education Access

Scholarships and learning support for children whose circumstances, not their ability, limit their schooling.

Community Health

Preventive health camps and access programmes in the communities where the group's businesses operate.

Rural Livelihood

Skill development and self-employment support that builds durable income, not one-time relief.

How the Foundation will work.

Structured, not occasional

The Foundation will run multi-year programmes with defined outcomes — philanthropy managed with the discipline of a business unit.

Local first

Initiatives begin in the communities surrounding our operations in Rajasthan and Gujarat, where our responsibility is most direct.

Accountable giving

Programme outcomes and fund utilisation will be reported annually with the same transparency we owe our shareholders.

Partner in purpose.

NGOs, institutions, and community organisations interested in working with the Foundation are invited to reach the group office.
