Real Estate

Properties built on trust, valuation built on transparency, partnerships built to last — across Jodhpur, Rajasthan, and growing.

Real Estate Division

A real estate practice grounded in transparency.

We acquire, sell, and manage residential and commercial property across Jodhpur and Rajasthan — with a turnover-sharing model that aligns our incentives with the people we work for.

  • Residential and commercial property sales and acquisitions
  • Verified titles, clearances, and due diligence before every deal
  • Turnover-sharing rental management for property owners
  • Strategic land sourcing for development partners
  • Long-term joint ventures and co-investment structures

What we offer

Five ways we work with property.

Property Sales

Residential and commercial property acquisitions in Jodhpur and across Rajasthan, with thorough due diligence on every deal.

Land Acquisition

Strategic land sourcing for development projects with verified titles, clearances, and zoning support end-to-end.

Rental Management

Turnover-sharing model for property owners — we handle tenants, maintenance, and reporting so owners stay hands-off.

Development Pipeline

Carefully planned projects in growth corridors with a long-term hold thesis and disciplined capital deployment.

Investor Partnerships

Joint ventures and co-investment structures for institutional partners and family offices seeking Rajasthan exposure.

Buying, selling, or co-investing?

Reach out to discuss specific properties, development opportunities, or our rental management programme — we respond within one business day.
